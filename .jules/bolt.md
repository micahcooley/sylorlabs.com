## 2026-01-29 - Utility Module Side Effects\n**Learning:** Top-level `setInterval` in utility modules (like `lib/csrf.ts`) keeps the process alive in tests and scripts, and is inefficient for serverless environments.\n**Action:** Use lazy cleanup patterns (triggered on access/generation) with throttling (e.g., `maybeCleanup()` with a timestamp check) instead of background intervals.
